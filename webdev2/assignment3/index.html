Jonny's Odds & Ends

Located in the basement of The Flatiron Building, that's 175 5th Ave, New York, NY 10010. It's the triangular building right next to Madison Square Park, make sure you don't miss it!

Despite the exotic menu, our restaurant prides itself on its standard presentation. With a homey feel and friendly staff, you're sure to have a good time!

We serve only the finest exotic food! Everything* here is a delicacy! We source our food from all around the world, going places no man has gone before in search of flavor!

Jonny is a man who loves strange wares! Appearing as an ordinary Joe Schmoe, he's always on the hunt for the next big thing. And his love for the culture of the world is what inspired him to open such a risky restaurant!

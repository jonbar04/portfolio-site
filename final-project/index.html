       About us

Our mission is to identify the personal relationship people can build with inanimate objects. Whether it's a childhood toy, or something you've built over the years.

As someone who personally identifiers as a collector, I wanted to look into the minds of like-minded people, and see what it is that makes our brain work this way.

Three people were interviewed as research for the data on this website, and I'd like to give them my thanks for giving me their time. So thank you to Alexander Muniz, Roman Hunter, and Gabriel Mendez.

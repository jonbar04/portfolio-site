Milestone 1: Graybox

I started with a simple graybox. This was purely to map out the general feel of the environment. My scaling was way different initially, as I still hadn't set everything in stone. A lot of my models were oversized and eventually slimmed down. As a graybox, there's not really too much of note. Just some basic boxes mapping stuff out.

Computer Animation: Modeling Final Project

Final Completion

The finished product! I went for lighting showing that it was night time, so it's rather dark. There's only a few light sources. There's a few lanterns throughout the home, but the strongest light sources are the two windows and the fireplace. I honestly struggled quite a bit to find a good compromise for the fire effect, it ended up being just a cylindrical area light. I did drop in one of the fire fluid effects, but it is pretty hard to see through all of the light emitting from it.

Overall, I'm pretty satisfied with this! There's probably a ton of imperfections I could catch, I would try and make the beds more fluffy if I knew how to do it and not mess up all my UVs, but it's good enough for me. I feel like the lighting choices really help with the presentation of the scene. That and the framing of the shots. I'm most proud of the first and fourth shots, I think those are my favorite angles that I captured the scene with.

It took a lot of work to get this far. There were a ton of hurdles I had to jump through and a ton of new things I had to learn, but I enjoyed it! For my first major modeling project, I'm pretty damn proud of it.

Representation in Touhou Project

Conclusion

Despite being a series primarily maintained by one man, Touhou has done a good job at representation, primarily when it comes to female presentation. Characters aren't sexualized, and they exist to serve a deeper purpose rather than just romance or being an extension of another character. The series also has diverse characters in several aspects, such as characters from different faiths, representation of disabled people, and androgyny. However, the series is lacking in other areas, such as representation of people of color, so it's far from perfect. When it comes to these areas lacking representation, fans have taken it upon themselves to fill the role. People in massive online communities headcanon characters and ship characters together to see representation that they wish they could see officially recognized.
